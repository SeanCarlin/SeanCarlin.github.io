Personal Projects

These are some ideas I developed in my spare time while learning programming skills. Most projects use HTML5 Canvas and RequestAnimationFrame. Some will work on mobile, but for most a desktop is required.
classicbattleship.com
This is my version of the classic board game Battleship. Organise your fleet of ships and try to guess the location of your opponents vessels. The first person to sink all five of your opponent's ships wins. Built using NodeJS, Socket.io, SVG.js, HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.
Lunar Lander
An interpretation of the classic arcade game Lunar Lander. Using the arrow keys and space bar, control the orientation and thrust of a spaceship to a gentle landing on the lunar surface.
Prime Spiral
Also known as Ulam's Spiral, each pixel represents a natural number spiraling out from the centre of the screen. Those that are prime, are filled. Interesting diagonal patterns occur from quadratic equations which contain more primes than others.
Mandelbrot Set
The famous Mandelbrot set depicting the behaviour of complex numbers of modulus less than two under iteration.
John Conway's Game of Life
Depicts how complex behaviours in populations can arrive from simple rules. In each iteration, those cells with fewer than two neighbours, or more than three, die. Those with two or three neighbours survive.
Orbit
A to-scale simulation of a spacecraft during a trans-lunar injection. Using Newton's law of gravitation combined with accurate mass, radii, and gravitational constant.
Graphing Calculator
For plotting 2-D graphs. Use the "t" variable as time to animate the graph.
Vigenere Cipher
A simple encryption algorithm for coding and decoding messages using a secret key between two parties.
Barriers Game
One of my first attempts at a web game. Use the space bar to avoid the incoming barriers.
